What if you could paint with your phone?

Details

Experiments with Processing

Role

Self-led

Tools

Processing & the Tramontana Library

Thought

Inspired by my light painting experiments, I further wondered what tools could be used to paint on a computer screen. Some amount of reading on the internet led me to the Tramontana Library, developed by Pierluigi Dalla Rosa.

Very interestingly, a mobile phone device has a gyroscope that can be accessed via the Tramontana library.



Since this experiment was programmed on Processing, thereby making it a Java applet, I cannot embed an interactive prototype on this website.
